# Prompts

## Generate 3 new viz, 21 Mar 2026 (Claude Code - Sonnet 4.6)

Generate 3 new data visualizations that are useful and have never been seen before.

Think and plan systematically about how you could go about this process.
Search online as required and verify that your design has not been seen before. Discard if it has and pick another until you find 3 designs never seen before.
Explain what real-life dataset and purpose this visualization is uniquely suited for and why it's better for that combination than any other alternative.
Create realistic, rich synthetic data that will highlight the power of the visualization.

Generate the visualizations inside an interactive HTML slide deck, one slide each, with explanations (with the MAJORITY of the slide focusing on the visualization).

---

Some of the content is barely visible - mostly because of poor contrast, partly because of small font sizes. Improve the appearance.
Document the _entire_ process in a detailed set of appendix slides - covering your thought process, search process, design process, data selection & generation, failures and backtracking, post-design feedback and iteration, a blameless post-mortem, etc.
